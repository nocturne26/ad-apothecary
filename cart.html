How Order Requests Work

This is not a direct checkout page.

Your cart will be submitted to AD Apothecary as an order request. Availability, shipping or pickup, and payment details will be confirmed before your order is finalized.

Product totals shown do not include shipping costs. We will contact you with final amount due and to accept payment.

Payment types accepted are Venmo or Paypal.
